So what makes it special?

We at DoBots believe Internet-of-Things, or IoT, is the future! There are many great products available to create your own smart house, so why did we build another one? We set out to create the cheapest, modular solution on the market and with our background in software engineering, we think the Crownstone also takes the crown in terms of features:


Turning devices on and off using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), through phone or the internet.
Dimming the lights with your smartphone.
Measuring power consumption of lights/devices.
Localization of BLE enabled devices, like smartphones, Fitbits, etc..
Identifying devices based on their consumption patterns.
Applications like a childlock on certain devices when you're away from home and many more!

Built-in or plug-in

The Crownstone combines the finest of iBeacon technology with smart socket technology. As soon as you enter or leave a room or building every (dumb) device can react to your presence. Your life becomes more comfortable and you save money at the same time! Reducing standby power consumption saves 10% of a household energy bill (e.g. $2,200 in the US, €1.900 in Holland, every year).

The Crownstone comes in a modern built-in version that fits into any power outlet, as well as a plug-in version for American (110V) and European (230V) outlets (concepts shown on images).

Details

The Crownstone is integrated with smartphones as well as smartphone add-ons such as the Fitbit, or Pebble. It forms a mesh to communicate through the entire house, and a Raspberry PI can be used as a hub to the internet.

Its most interesting feature is that it is able to recognize devices by their power profile. You do not need to configure that you use a curling iron for a particular socket. It will recognize this and automatically turn it off, when you forget to unplug it after use.

Open source

The Crownstone embedded software is open source and already placed online at Github!

For the maker community who wants to use Crownstones to create their own smart building, we provide a full-featured API you can access through Bluetooth.

This is not required! Crownstones come with a lot of functionality so everyone can use it out of the box, without having to do any coding themselves.
