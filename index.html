Crownstone, coming soon!

"Every wall socket made smart!"
"My house goes on standby when I leave"

Built-in or plug-in

The Crownstone combines the finest of iBeacon technology with smart socket technology. As soon as you enter or leave a room or building every (dumb) device can react to your presence. Your life becomes more comfortable and you save money at the same time! Reducing standby power consumption saves 10% of a household energy bill (e.g. $2,200 in the US, €1.900 in Holland, every year).

The Crownstone comes in a modern built-in version that fits into any power outlet, as well as a plug-in version for American (110V) and European (230V) outlets (concept shown on image).

Features

The Crownstone is integrated with smartphones as well as smartphone add-ons such as the FitBit, or Pebble. It forms a mesh to communicate through the entire house, and a Raspberry PI can be used as a hub to the internet.

It's most interesting feature is that it is able to recognize devices by their power profile. You do not need to configure that you use a curling iron for a particular socket. It will recognize this and automatically turn it off, when you forget to unplug it after use.

Open source

The Crownstone embedded software is open-source and already placed online at github!

For the maker community who want to use Crownstones to create your own smart building, we provide a full-featured API you can access through Bluetooth.

This is ofcourse not required! Crownstones come with a lot of functionality so everyone can use it without having do any coding themselves.
